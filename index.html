Fun Facts

My name is Orion and my favorite animal is the elephant.
They can grow up to 13 feet tall, and weigh up to 14,000 pounds.
Elephants are excellent swimmers, have very thick skin and are super smart!

If you want to learn more about elephants, check out their wikipedia page.

Elephants are awesome because they...

  1. Can use tools
  2. Have excellent memories
  3. Mourn their dead
  4. Can identify different languages

If I were an elephant, I would...

  • Splash around in a river
  • Throw things with my trunk
  • Talk to other elephants
